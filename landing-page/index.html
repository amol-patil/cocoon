Why Cocoon?

⚡

Instant Access

Summon the desktop overlay with a global shortcut. Search, copy, and dismiss — all in under two seconds.

🔍

Fuzzy Search

Find documents by name, category, owner, or any field value. Powered by Fuse.js with smart highlighting.

📱

Desktop + Mobile

macOS overlay for your desk, iOS app in your pocket. Same encrypted export format works on both.

📋

One-Click Copy

Copy any field to the clipboard instantly. Auto-clear after a configurable timeout for extra security.

🏷️

Organized

Categories, owners, custom fields, and temporary items. Structure your documents however you want.

🔗

File Links

Link entries to scanned PDFs or images on your drive. Open the original document with one click.

Built for privacy

Your data never leaves your devices. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
